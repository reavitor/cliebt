Maintenance program

Our Maintenance Programs


Did you know that having preventative maintenance on you HVAC unit will prevent spending money on hefty repairs, prolong the life of your unit, and save you money on energy bills?


Air conditioning maintenance



  1. Filter changes

  2. Thermostat operation

  3. Check up on basic electrical components

  4. Check charge levels and pressure on the unit

  5. Hose down condenser coils

  6. Check indoor temperatures

  7. Take amp draw on fan motors and compressor

  8. Inspect air ducts

Heating Maintenance



  1. Change filter

  2. Thermostat operation

  3. Clean flame sensor

  4. Check for gas leaks

  5. Carbon monoxide detection

  6. Inspect proper burner flame

  7. Take amp draw on blower motor

  8. Inspect air ducts
