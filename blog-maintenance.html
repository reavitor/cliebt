Heating maintenance and safety

Introduction

August 1, 2019

Furnaces are gas fired heaters, with gas there are a lot of important safety hazards. When your heater runs on gas you are at risk of gas leaks from the gas line, as well as carbon monoxide, which comes from the heaters heat exchanger. Heating maintenance is important so we can detect any possible hazards that may put you at risk, such as looking for gas leaks, checking for carbon monoxide leaks. The main reason for this maintenance is safety.
